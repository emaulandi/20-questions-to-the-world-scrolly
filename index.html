How it all started

It all started from a simple and yet complex question : How to understand the world?

It is so easy to be either fascinated or deeply worried about the way our world is evolving. We are polluting it like there’s no tomorrow, but at the same time more and more people have access to safe food and water. There has never been so few conflicts and wars but at the same time.

The journey

Since 2016, we keep asking the same 20 open questions to hundreds of people worldwide, from more than 80 nationalities, and very social backgrounds.

For you, what is the main characteristic that all human beings have in common ? Is your life easier or more complicated than your parents' life ? And 18 more questions, to better know the people we meet, and their views of the world.

See the first journey we took to meet a great variety of people.

Departure country was France

In each country, interviews were conducted. One dot represent one people met and interviewed.

Then the world tour began in Europe

And all over the world, to meet a great variety of people

All over, 450 people were met and answered the 20 questions.

Based on the 10,000+ answers that we already collected, we share fresh and inspiring video content showing a great diversity of opinions, and we design artistic concepts. Our goal is to create a feeling of global empathy, a necessary step to solve our global problems.
